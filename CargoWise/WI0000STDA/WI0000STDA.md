##### **Update Note**

# Viewing another test UPN ready for review

## Advanced Order Manager

##### 25 September 2025  

***

### Summary

Supplier bookings with the load modes ‘Other’ and ‘Loose Cargo’ along with their associated records are now visible in the Order Explorer (OE) grid.

### Description

Supplier bookings with load modes of ‘Other’ or ‘Loose Cargo’ and their associated Advanced Order Manager (AOM) records were not available in Order Explorer (OE) grid.

> [!IMPORTANT]
Supplier bookings with load modes of ‘Other’ or ‘Loose Cargo’ used to be known as Loose Cargo (LSE). Refer to Simplified Load Mode in NEO portal for the load mode’s previous naming conventions.

They have now been added in this update with the addition of the following non-default sortable
columns and filters:

- Dispatched Qty + UOM
- Dispatched Packages + UOM
